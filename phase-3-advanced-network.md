# Phase 3: Advanced Network

Phase 3 should expand Secant from a payment app into a broader merchant network and integration layer.

## Advanced Integrations

- Shopify app.
- WooCommerce plugin.
- Stripe-compatible payment intent API.
- Slack and Discord invoice bots.
- Telegram checkout bot.
- API keys and webhook replay for developers.
- Hosted SDK widgets for checkout and invoice status.
- Mobile merchant app for live PoS use.

## Partnerships

Potential partnership areas:

- Wallets for one-click Secant checkout support.
- On-ramp and off-ramp providers.
- Stablecoin issuers and payment networks.
- Accounting platforms.
- Solana ecosystem programs and Superteam distribution.
- Merchant platforms and commerce builders.
- Regional payout providers.

## Advanced Payment Features

- Recurring billing and subscriptions.
- Usage-based billing.
- Payment links with customer identity.
- Cross-border payroll.
- Multi-recipient split payments.
- Escrow-style milestone payments.
- Smart routing across Base, Solana, and future rails.
- Automatic customer token-to-USDC conversion at checkout.

## Treasury Features

- Merchant treasury policy rules.
- Auto-sweep to preferred wallet.
- Stablecoin allocation by chain.
- Optional idle-balance routing to approved treasury products.
- FX and stablecoin exposure reporting.
- Risk limits by wallet, chain, and counterparty.

## Enterprise Readiness

- Organizations and workspaces.
- Role-based access control.
- Approval policies.
- Audit logs.
- Webhook retry policies and dead-letter queues.
- SLA monitoring.
- White-label checkout.
- Dedicated settlement webhooks.
